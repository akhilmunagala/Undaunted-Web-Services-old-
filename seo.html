What actually SEO is?

SEO stands for SEARCH ENGINE OPTIMIZATION. Well that is somewhat confusing for complete beginners so, let us break it down so that you will understand this concept a little deeper.

Search:

Well this is what you ask for google or any other search engine, remember you will just say hey google “what is this” and “what is that?”, this questions are all called queries. These queries consist of different words to form a sentence of course, but for search engines, they are just bunch of different Keywords.

Engine:

Search Engines are engines, which crawl different databases available on the internet and present results in an ordered fashion according to their page ranking.

Optimization:

Optimization is making your website friendly with search engines so that they can rank you high in their searches.

Why is it important for your business?

Imagine yourself googling for some stuff and google presents you a list of results that it finds best, and you would have just clicked on top 5 results, and I can guarantee you that you’ve almost never visited the other pages that are listed right. Well that is what happens if you do not optimize your websites for search engines, they just ignore you and you will starve to death with no traffic at all.

By optimizing your websites or pages for search engines, they will show you high on their search results, such that you will be getting traffic that is more organic and saving you tons of money on the paid advertising.
