What is marketing?

This is the most important word for your business that you never forget even in your sleep, why do you ask, because this is what gets you sales. If you do not have this formula then no matter how great product/service do you have it is set up to DOOM. Because this is what puts your product or service in front of your customers to buy.

What is digital marketing?

Well this is also marketing but, in a digital way, because in today’s world more than 90% of population are online, selling and buying stuff, which means there’s more potential for you to grow your business more by targeting them.

What is the difference between them?

Traditional marketing means doing advertising in TV’s, radio’s etc. Where it reaches your customers but this technique is more kind of a blind, because you do not know who are going to buy and you are not getting their contact information so that you can send promotional offers to make more money. On the other hand, digital marketing solves all of these problems providing you the medium to target people who are more likely to buy your product or services, instead of showing your ad to everyone. which means saving a ton on ad’s and you’ll get their contact information, which is more valuable to you because those are the people who are interested in you and will buy from you again and again.

Why should you care about it?

It is simple if you do not know who your audience are then you will sell nothing, even if it is the world’s best product you will earn nothing.
